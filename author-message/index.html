Dear Reader,

Welcome to HOTS. In a time of information overload and algorithmic noise, we set out to build something different: a calm, rigorous, and beautifully crafted news experience focused on substance over speed.

Every story we publish is chosen because it matters — whether it’s a breakthrough in AI governance that will shape the next decade, a diplomatic development that could prevent conflict, or a strange discovery that reminds us how little we still understand about our planet.

We believe journalism can be both urgent and thoughtful. Our reporters work without the pressure of clickbait or outrage cycles. Our design is deliberately minimal so the words and ideas remain the focus.

Thank you for reading. We hope HOTS becomes a trusted part of your daily or weekly information diet.

— The HOTS Editorial Team
