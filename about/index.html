About HOTS

HOTS (Hot Off The Press) is a premium digital news publication committed to delivering sharp, independent, and deeply reported journalism on the stories that matter most.

Founded as part of the WeirdHub network, HOTS covers breaking news, world affairs, technology, artificial intelligence, science, business, culture, and the strange and unexpected corners of our world with clarity and rigor.

Our Principles

  • Independence — We are not owned by any government, corporation, or political interest.
  • Clarity — We explain complex issues without oversimplifying.
  • Global Perspective — We report from every continent with local insight and international context.
  • Future Focus — We pay special attention to technology, AI, space, and climate — the forces shaping the decades ahead.

Our journalists and contributors are based in major capitals and remote outposts alike. We believe great reporting requires both intellectual honesty and genuine curiosity about how the world actually works.
